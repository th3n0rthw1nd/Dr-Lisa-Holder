About Dr. Holder

A therapist who walks with you, not ahead of you.

Dr. Lisa C. Holder has been devoted to the mental health field for nearly three decades, building a private practice rooted in warmth, confidentiality, and genuine respect for each person's unique story. She has been happily married for over 40 years and brings that lived perspective into her work with couples and families.

Originally from Tennessee, Dr. Holder grew up in a military family and lived all across the United States and abroad, giving her a broad understanding of diverse life experiences. She settled in Colorado Springs in 1997, where she established her private practice, and now also resides in Florida.

Whether you have struggled for weeks, months, or years, Dr. Holder offers encouragement, evidence-based guidance, and genuine commitment to walking beside you on your path forward.

Therapeutic Services

Specialized care for life's most complex challenges.

Treatment is designed specifically for each individual. Sessions run 45 to 50 minutes. Video and phone sessions are available with flexible hours, from the comfort of your own home.

Individual Therapy

One-on-one sessions providing a confidential, non-judgmental space to explore personal challenges, process emotions, and build healthier coping strategies.

Marriage & Couples

Evidence-based support for navigating communication breakdowns, rebuilding trust, and strengthening the bond between partners. Strong marriages create happy, well-adjusted families.

Child, Teen & Family

Therapeutic support for children, adolescents, and entire families navigating stress, blended family dynamics, divorce, or major life transitions.

Trauma & EMDR

Highly trained in Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing (EMDR II), a proven, FDA-recognized approach to resolving trauma, PTSD, stressful experiences, and abuse. Available remotely.

Somatic & Psycho-Physiology

Body-awareness and psychophysiology techniques, including Somatic Therapy, that help heal the nervous system and release the buried effects of trauma on mind and body.

Anxiety & Depression

Structured, compassionate CBT and insight-oriented therapy to address anxiety disorders, depression, mood disorders, and the chronic emotional weight that keeps life feeling impossible.

Philosophy & Approach

How healing actually happens.

01

Tailored, not templated

Dr. Holder selects therapeutic methods specifically for each patient. CBT, EMDR, somatic techniques, and insight-oriented therapy are applied where they do the most good, calibrated to your history and your goals, not a standardized script.

02

Buried pain has a way forward

One of Dr. Holder's core clinical observations across nearly three decades: past pain, left unaddressed, shapes our present and future. Uncovering and working through those embedded experiences is often the key that frees people for happiness and healthier relationships today.

03

Warm, non-judgmental, and goal-oriented

The therapeutic relationship Dr. Holder builds is both compassionate and purposeful. You will feel heard and supported, while also moving toward real, trackable goals. Empathy and accountability are not opposites here.

04

A genuine belief in your potential

Behind every session is a foundational conviction: every individual truly has the capacity to grow through extreme difficulty, manage life more productively, and build something full of hope and joy. That is not a platitude. It is the operating premise of this practice.
