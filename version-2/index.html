About Dr. Holder

A compassionate clinical presence with decades of experience.

Reaching out for help may be the hardest part of the therapeutic journey. Dr. Holder’s work centers on creating a safe, nonjudgmental space where clients can speak honestly, understand old patterns, and learn healthier tools for daily life and relationships.

Dr. Holder works with people of many backgrounds and concerns, including trauma, relationship pain, depression, anxiety, grief, self-esteem issues, abuse histories, stress, and life transitions.

Her style is warm, holistic, and solution-oriented. She combines clinical training with practical tools, empathy, and deep respect for each person’s ability to grow through extreme difficulty and heal from past wounds.

She describes the therapeutic process as walking alongside her clients through life’s ups and downs while offering support, insight, self-awareness, and healthy coping strategies.

“Every person has strengths to overcome their current situation, with support, insight, and better self-awareness.”

Therapeutic Approach

Clinical skill without losing the human warmth.

Dr. Holder’s approach combines mind, body, and heart. Sessions are designed to be compassionate and useful, giving clients a place to be deeply heard while also learning tools that can change daily life.

01

Safety first

Therapy begins with a confidential, nonjudgmental space where the client can speak honestly and feel emotionally safe enough to look at what hurts.

02

Personalized treatment

No two clients are treated the same. Dr. Holder selects from EMDR, CBT, somatic tools, IFS, relationship work, and insight-oriented methods based on the client’s needs.

03

Healthy tools

Clients are supported emotionally while also learning coping strategies, communication tools, self-awareness, and healthier patterns for relationships and stress.

04

Hope with direction

The goal is not only to talk about pain. The goal is to help clients move toward healing, stronger relationships, clarity, and a life that feels less stuck.
